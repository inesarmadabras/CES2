MySNS Recommended Apps



MySNS Recommended Apps is our opportunity to share mobile applications developed by citizens and other organizations that, in our view, play a significant role in bringing citizens closer to their health.
The content and performance of the recommended applications are entirely the responsibility of the manufacturer.
If you would like to see your application published on this page, send us an email to nma@spms.min-saude.pt with the following information:
- Application name
- Description
- Author / Developer
- Link to Apple App Store / Google Play Store

Technical Description:
- What technologies were used?
- What measures does the app take to provide security of user input and to authenticate the user?
- What steps have been taken to validate the operation of the app on each platform?

The recommended applications should comply with the 'EU guidelines on assessment of the reliability of mobile health applications' and the Code of Conduct on privacy for mobile health applications, also defined by the EC.
