About Mission



The MySNS Community is an initiative carried out by the Shared Services of the Ministry of Health - SPMS EPE, whose mission is to make available and fully accessible the debugging and development of mobile health applications.

To foster organizational synergy and knowledge sharing, and without forgetting the need to protect citizens' information and privacy, MySNS Community seeks to be a collaborative platform for all citizens, health professionals and developers, promoting the sharing of information related to the technologies used in the development, as well as making our open source projects available, ensuring transparency and the knowledge exchange.

For more information about Open Source projects, we strongly recommend reading the European Commission strategy for internal use of Open Source Software.


Structure

The MySNS Community is divided essentially into 3 sections: Citizens, HealthCare Professional and Developer..
In the area dedicated to the Citizens & HealthCare Professionals, beyond the dedicated documentation, the users will have access to an open discussion forum for query handling, bug report & suggestions.
Although the forum is mediated, the posts are the sole responsibility of their writers. The forum follows the Code of Conduct, available on the Terms and Conditions.
